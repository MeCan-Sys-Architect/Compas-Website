MBSE Infrastructure Set-Up

We set up the tools and systems you need to make Model-Based Systems Engineering work smoothly in your organization. This includes:

  • Connecting with PLM systems to manage data and documents
  • Linking with engineering tools like CAD and MATLAB/Simulink
  • Organizing databases to keep models and information in one place

Example: For a recent aerospace and defense client, we demonstrated the integration
of Capella MBSE with JIRA, Git, and CAD tools.

This setup enabled rapid and automated document generation, improving project delivery speed and ensuring consistency across engineering teams.

Our goal is to create an efficient, connected environment that supports teamwork and keeps your projects on track.
