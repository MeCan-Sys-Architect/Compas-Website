Frequently Asked Questions

What is Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE)? +
MBSE is a formalized methodology used to support the requirements, design, analysis, verification, and validation of complex systems. Instead of relying on documents, it uses integrated system models as the primary source of truth, improving communication, reducing errors, and accelerating development.
How can MBSE benefit my startup or small tech team? +
For agile teams, MBSE provides a structured yet flexible framework to manage complexity and ensure all stakeholders share a common understanding of the system. It helps catch design flaws early, ensures requirements are met, and streamlines the path from concept to a market-ready product, saving you time and resources.
What tools and technologies do you specialize in? +
We are proficient in a range of industry-standard tools, including Capella, Cameo Systems Modeler (with SysML), MATLAB/Simulink for simulations, and various PLM platforms. We tailor the toolchain to fit your specific project needs and existing infrastructure.
What does your 'No COST!' architecture setup include? +
Our complimentary initial setup is designed to give you a tangible starting point. It typically includes a foundational system model, a basic requirements hierarchy, and a demonstration of how the core components of your system interact. It's a risk-free way to see the value of MBSE for your project.
