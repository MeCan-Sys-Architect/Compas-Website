Frequently Asked Questions

What is MBSE and why is it important? +
MBSE (Model-Based Systems Engineering) is a systems engineering approach that uses models to support system requirements, design, analysis, and verification.
Do you support Aerospace & Defense sectors? +
Yes, we offer MBSE, toolchain integration, and lifecycle support tailored for Aerospace and Defense domains.
Is the first consultation free? +
Absolutely. We offer a no-cost initial MBSE architecture setup to help you get started.
Do you provide training services? +
Yes, we conduct training sessions on MBSE tools, process development, and verification & validation strategies.
What tools do you integrate? +
We support tools like Simulink, MATLAB, Polarion, Capella, Enterprise Architect, and various PLM systems.
